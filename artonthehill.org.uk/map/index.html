Bus:

75, 76 and 90 run between North Bristol and South Bristol stopping at/near Malago Road.

50 from Cabot Circus to Hengrove stops at the junction of St Johns Lane and Redcatch Rd.

 

Train:

Nearest station is Bedminster Station, be aware that trains on Sunday are less regular.

 

Car:

Car parks on Hereford St and in Little Paradise, both just off Malago Rd. There is street parking on Whitehouse Road and Philip St.

Windmill Hill and Victoria Park are residential areas with narrow streets with limited space for parking. Please be considerate to residents.
