Art on the Hill AGM - Thur 11-Feb at 7:30pm

Held this year virtually, via Zoom!

The AOTH Committee are hoping to be able to put together some kind of trail in October 2021 to be able to showcase what the incredible community have been up to in lockdown!

This year's AGM will take place virtually, so please do register to join us below. You may be interested in exhibiting this year, you may want to suggest some ideas for how the trail could move forward, or maybe you'd like to volunteer to join the committee? Click below to register for the AGM:

Register

If you are interested in taking part in any way, to send a mail to takepartaoth@gmail.com to register your interest, and we will be able to follow up in a few months when the picture is clearer.

We're really looking forward to seeing what art/songs/ideas you're working on during lockdown!

 

More than just an arts trail!

In addition to many exciting artists exhibiting in their own homes and community spaces, there will be workshops for children, a treasure hunt, and live music throughout the weekend.

This year's trail will be on the 3rd and 4th of October 2020.

Check out our social media accounts for news and pictures of previous years' trails.

The trail is organised by a team of volunteers, if you would like to help out with the organisation of the trail next year, please do not hesitate to get in touch - we look forward to hearing from you!

We are particularly interested in anyone who would like to be a committee secretary, which is an important job for us and a great opportunity to meet people in your local community.
